Welcome to Mayhem Barbell

Mayhem Barbell is a strength and conditoning training facility founded by two nationally accredited powerlifting coaches. It is a 24/7 operated gym located in Iowa that is catered to powerlifters, bodybuilders, and anyone who is interested in strength sports. Mayhem also offers both in person and online coaching for all levels.

ATMOSPHERE

In Mayhem Barbell, most of our athletes compete on a national level and these guys have one goal in mind, and that is to be the BEST of the BEST. The atmosphere in this gym will truly push you to your limits and make you become a better version of yourself.

EQUIPMENT

We have everything you need here at Mayhem, from standard gym equipment to competition grade equipment. We have 11000 pounds worth of plates for everyone to enjoy and make sure you'll never spend time in the gym waiting for equipment.

STRONG SUPPORT

We get that most beginners get intimidated asking for help in the gym. Lucky for you, our staffs here at MAYHEM are also national level athletes and are more than willing to help with the basics for FREE. We also offer in depth 1 on 1 and online coaching.
