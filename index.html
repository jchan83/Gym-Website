Welcome to Mayhem Barbell

Mayhem Barbell is a 24/7 facility catered to powerlifters, bodybuilders, and anyone who is passionate in any form of strength sports. In Mayhem Barbell, there is always enough equipment for YOU. The gym consists of multiple combo racks, power cages, rogue benches, calibrated and cast iron plates.

Interested in Coaching?

Why workout here

ATMOSPHERE

In Mayhem, most of our athletes here compete on a national level and they have one goal and that is to be the best. The atmosphere in this gym will push you to your limits and make you become a better version of yourself.

EQUIPMENT

We have everything you need here at Mayhem, from standard gym equipment to competition grade equipment. We have 11000 pounds worth of plates for everyone to enjoy and make sure you'll never spend time in the gym waiting for equipment.

STRONG SUPPORT

We get that most beginners get intimidated asking for help in the gym. Lucky for you, our staffs here at MAYHEM are also national level athletes and are more than willing to help with the basics for FREE. We also offer in depth 1 on 1 and online coaching
